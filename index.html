Planning Help

Travel

Fly into La Aurora International Airport (GUA) in Guatemala City, then plan about one hour for the scenic drive to Antigua.

Flights

Fly into Guatemala City via La Aurora International Airport. We recommend arriving at least one day early to settle in and enjoy Antigua.

3–6 hr flight time Direct or 1 stop Best arrival: Thu–Fri

July Travel Insight

July is peak summer travel. Morning arrivals tend to be smoother, and brief afternoon showers are common but pass quickly.

Airport Code: GUA Explore Flights

Transportation

We recommend arranging a private driver or shuttle from La Aurora International Airport to Antigua. This is the most comfortable and reliable option for guests.

~1 hour drive Private or shared shuttle Pre-arranged recommended

What to Expect

Airport transfers are common and reliable. Drivers are professional and familiar with the route to Antigua.

Arrival Tip

Daytime arrivals are easiest, though evening arrivals are perfectly fine when transportation is arranged in advance.

Hotels

Antigua offers a beautiful selection of boutique hotels and restored colonial properties. We recommend booking early to ensure the best availability.

Historic Center Boutique Courtyards Quiet Retreats

Booking Tip

Staying within walking distance of Antigua’s historic center makes getting around easy and allows you to fully enjoy the city before and after the celebration.

Guest Experience

Antigua is compact, walkable, and guest-friendly. Most hotels offer front desk assistance, transportation coordination, and luggage support.

Things to Do

Explore Antigua at your own pace, from cobblestone streets and historic landmarks to local markets and coffee farms.

Coffee Tours Local Markets Scenic Viewpoints Historic Sites Local Cuisine

Getting Around

Antigua is best explored on foot. Most attractions, restaurants, and shops are within walking distance of the historic center.
